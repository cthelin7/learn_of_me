 Where do these come from?

Several years ago, Russell M. Nelson encouraged people to study everything in the scriptures concerning what Jesus said, did, and who He is. I wrote down every new thing I learned about Christ - attributes and qualities of His personality, how He sees things, what He finds important, who He is, etc. I learned so much about my Savior, and feel I know Him better. I also know the Book of Mormon is a book inspired of God. It doesn't just mention Christ, but helps us know Him.

This page displays a random selection from that study. Take a second to think about each one: what does it mean to you, that Jesus is like this? Does it help you see Him as a real, living person?
